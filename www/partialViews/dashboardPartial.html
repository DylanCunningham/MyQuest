MyQuest Game Instructions

You have been asked to complete a secret mission by Agent Blue, who is working undercover in Student and Library Services. He has left a secret message for you to uncover that will be vital for you to succeed. Complete the Quest and uncover the clues to find the message.


In order to complete the quest, you will need to read a clue, then find the location around the campus. You will know you have found the clue, as there will be a QR code. Scan the QR code for the next clue!
