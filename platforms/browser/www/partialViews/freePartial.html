Join our alumni association

When you graduate from Teesside University you become a free, lifetime member of our alumni association. Join today to gain access to our careers service and business support, exclusive invites to events throughout the year, e-newsletters and annual Teesside Graduate magazine and unlimited use of our online careers portal to help you succeed in the world of work.

Counselling

The counsellor listens to you carefully in a supportive and respectful way, without judging you. The counsellor helps you explore and clarify issues and develop more constructive ways of dealing with them. This may mean developing an 'Action Plan' to tackle the issues.

Counselling is a service made available to students free of charge by the University.
